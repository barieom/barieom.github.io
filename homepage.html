My Big Project

I am launching the TAMID–Keyrus Data Science Group on the fall of 2018. I conceived the idea of this organization when I realized that most college campuses lack mediums for students to explore data science. Rather than taking online classes on my own, I decided to take initiative by creating a scalable national data science organization. I first persistently reached out to Keyrus, ultimately establishing a partnership with the international firm to incept the TAMID–Keyrus Data Science Group. I created the data science education curriculum with the help of Keyrus and resourced data case projects, Vistaprint being one of the clients. Now, the group is launched at Tufts University, with plans to expand nationally next year.
